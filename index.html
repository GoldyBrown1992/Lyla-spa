frequently asked questions

How often should my head spa ritual be?

We recommend once a month to nourish your scalp, awaken hair follicles, and immerse yourself in deep relaxation.

Do I need to wash my hair before my visit?

Not at all — we'll take care of everything for you.

Do you use professional products?

Yes, we use premium haircare infused with aromatherapy for a truly soothing experience.

Is it safe during pregnancy or while breastfeeding?

Absolutely. Head Spa is a gentle and restorative ritual. We adjust the massage bed for your comfort, making it especially nurturing during pregnancy and postpartum.

Can I dry my hair after the treatment?

Yes, a blow dryer and comb are available in the treatment room. To ensure a serene flow for all guests, we kindly ask to limit drying time to 10 minutes. ✨
